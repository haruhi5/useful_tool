# Hiking Safety Guide

A comprehensive hiking safety checklist and detailed guidelines in Chinese and English (徒步安全事项指南).

## Features

- **Quick Checklist** - Essential items before and during hiking
- **Detailed Guidelines** - In-depth safety information
- **Print-Friendly** - Easy to print and carry on hikes
- **Organized Sections** - Weather, terrain, gear, wildlife, emergency
- **Mobile-Friendly** - Responsive design for reading on phone

## Contents

### Quick Checklist
- Weather & Environment
- Vegetation & Terrain
- Health & Supplies
- Navigation & Time
- Essential Gear
- Wildlife & Insects
- Water Crossings
- Communication & Emergency

### Detailed Sections
1. **Pre-Hike Preparation** - Planning and fitness checks
2. **Essential Gear Checklist** - Complete gear list with 14+ items
3. **During the Hike** - Pacing, breaks, group safety
4. **Weather Hazards** - Lightning, heat, cold, floods
5. **Injury Prevention** - Ankle sprains, blisters, dehydration
6. **Wildlife Encounters** - Bears, snakes, ticks, insects
7. **Emergency Procedures** - What to do if something goes wrong

## How to Use

1. **Review Before** - Read through the quick checklist before your hike
2. **Print It** - Click "Print / 打印" to print for reference
3. **Carry It** - Keep the printout in your backpack
4. **Check Emergency** - Review emergency procedures if needed

## Key Safety Principles

- ✅ Life safety is the #1 priority
- ✅ Share your itinerary with someone
- ✅ Bring enough water and food
- ✅ Start early to avoid hiking in darkness
- ✅ Set a "turnaround time" and stick to it
- ✅ Never hike alone if possible
- ✅ Check weather before departing

## Emergency Summary

If something goes wrong:
1. **Stay calm** - Panic clouds judgment
2. **Assess** - Evaluate the injury/situation
3. **Call for help** - Use phone/whistle with location
4. **Stay in place** - Don't wander off trail
5. **First aid** - Apply treatment; prevent infection
6. **Stay warm** - Use emergency blanket
